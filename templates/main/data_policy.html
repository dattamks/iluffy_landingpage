Data Usage Policy

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

This policy explains how i-Luffy processes, stores, and protects the data you provide — specifically your resumes and job descriptions.

1. What Data We Process

  • Resume files (PDF) — parsed for text extraction and analysis.
  • Job descriptions — used solely for keyword matching and scoring.
  • Analysis results — scores, feedback, and AI-generated suggestions.

2. Processing Environment

All resume analysis is performed in isolated, ephemeral sandboxes. Each analysis session runs in its own container that is destroyed immediately after processing completes. No data persists in the processing environment beyond the session.

3. AI Model Training

Your data is never used to train our AI models. We use pre-trained language models that are fine-tuned on synthetic and publicly available resume data only. Your personal resume content remains exclusively yours.

4. Data Storage

  • Resume files and analysis results are stored encrypted (AES-256) on secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Data is stored in data centers compliant with SOC 2 Type II standards.
  • Backups are encrypted and rotated every 7 days.

5. Data Deletion

  • You can delete individual analyses or resumes at any time from your dashboard.
  • Deleting your account permanently removes all associated data within 30 days.
  • Deleted data is purged from backups within 90 days.

6. Third-Party Access

No third party has access to your resume content. Our AI processing uses API calls to language model providers, but only anonymized text fragments are transmitted — never your full resume or personal identifiers.

7. Shared Reports

When you share an analysis report via link (Pro feature), the report is accessible only to users with the exact link. You can revoke shared links at any time. Shared reports do not include your original resume file.
