Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use i-Luffy, we collect the following information:

  • Account data: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Resume data: The resume files you upload for analysis.
  • Job descriptions: Text or URLs you provide for matching.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, timestamps, and device/browser information.
  • Payment data: Processed securely by our payment provider; we do not store card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the resume analysis service.
  • To generate AI-powered feedback, scores, and recommendations.
  • To send transactional emails (analysis results, account notifications).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers for hosting and processing.
  • Payment processors to handle subscriptions.
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Resume files and analysis results can be deleted at any time from your dashboard. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest), isolated processing sandboxes, and regular security audits to protect your data.
